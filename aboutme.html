Favorite Book: Ready Player One

Ready Player One is my favorite book because it takes all the things I am into
like video games, music, and movies and tells a very interesting adventure story.
It is about a VR world that people go to in the future to instead of being in the
real world. It references real games like 'joust' and movies like the 'Ghostbusters'.
So it was a treat to keep reading and seeing all the references I could get.
