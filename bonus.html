Superhero: Navy Blue Bear

Navy Blue Bear is a bear that has gifts with water and being able
to do things with water that no one can. He is also very strong
and has the ability to summon water in his hands. He also can talk
to any animal that resides in water.

Three Powers Navy Blue Bear Has:

  • Water Bending
  • Super Strength
  • Can communicate with aquatic animals
